Our Mission

ATDev is dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through innovative assistive technology solutions. We believe that everyone deserves access to tools that enhance their quality of life and independence.

Our flagship product, the Reflex Smart Knee Brace, represents our commitment to combining cutting-edge technology with human-centered design to create meaningful impact in post-surgical rehabilitation.
